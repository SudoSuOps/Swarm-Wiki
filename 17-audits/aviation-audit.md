## Competitive Differentiation

**Opportunity**: Regulatory AI for FAA compliance is underserved. No one is building fine-tuned models for:
- Safety compliance documentation
- Flight operations analysis
- Pilot training scenarios
- MRO (Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul)
- ATC (Air Traffic Control) procedures

**Challenge**: 45K pairs is thin. Need 200K+ for a competitive standalone model.
